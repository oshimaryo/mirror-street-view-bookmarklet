Mirror Street View

Description

This bookmarklet allows you to mirror the Street View panorama.

Installation

  • 1. Drag the following link to your bookmarks bar: Mirror Street View
  • 2. Click the bookmarklet when you're on a Street View page.
  • 3. Click the bookmarklet again to unmirror the panorama.

Limitations

There are a few limitations to this bookmarklet:

  • It only works on Street View pages.
  • You can only go forward or backward.
  • You can turn right or left.
